The US is one of the biggest tourist destinations for good reasons. It's vast and diverse. It's regions are unique and everyone will be able to find a place that they love coming back to every year. In May 2019, my wife and I spent 44 days and drove 12,000 miles around the US

The whole country is beautiful and it's easy to find great stops everywhere from Ohio to Texas. However, these 10 places are our highlights. There's mountains and deserts. There's hectic crowded urban jungles and there's remote desolate mountains. All 10 are one of a kind.

New York, NY

Every city lover and foodie's dream! New York is big, vibrant, and energetic. With every kind of food imaginable, you can eat your heart out and then relax at Central Park or shopping in Midtown.

Rocky Mountain National Park, CO

Rocky Mountain National Park, located within an hour drive of Denver and Boulder is a wonderful retreat to the mountains. Even in the middle of summer, visitors can find snow-capped peaks and emerald blue lakes to relax for some family friendly fun.

North West Wyoming

Wyoming is a breathtakingly beautiful state and the north western part of the state is the pinnacle of beauty. Packed into the north western part of Wyoming, you have Yellowstone National Park, Grand Tetons Nation Park, and the Wind River Range. What separates Wyoming from other mountain destinations is the seclusion. If you're in parts of the Wind River Range, you can go hiking for days without coming into contact with a single person.

Olympic National Park, WA

Olympic National Park, just west of Seattle, is home to a diverse ecosystem. In one park, it boasts mountain ranges, pebbly beaches, and temperate rain forests. If you want to get out and explore, this diverse park allows you to get lost and celebrate how diverse our country is.

San Francisco, CA

Home to San Francisco, San Jose, and many of the largest companies in tech. It's hip and trendy. There are many reasons to visit the Bay Area and you can pack a lot into your trip.

Horseshoe Bend & Antelope Canyon, AZ

Antelope Canyon and Horseshoe Bend are located in Northern Arizona. These beautiful desert destinations have surprisingly gorgeous photos. Great for the photography lover in your family!

Grand Canyon, AZ

The Grand Canyon has been a popular destination in the US and for good reason. With stunning views and gorges, it should be on everyone's bucketlist.

White Sands National Park, NM

White Sands National Park, located 45 minutes NE of Las Cruces, NM, is worth a visit if you're passing through. Sparkling white sand stretches out as far as you can see against a backdrop of stunning mountains. You can hike, drive through the sand, go sledding down sand dunes, or have a cookout. Just don't go mid-day during the summer.

Okefenokee Swamp, GA

Okefenokee Swamp, located in SW Georgia, runs swamp tours on pontoon boats. You'll get a view of the swamp, habitat, and of course gators!

Outer Banks, NC

Crossing into the Outer Banks, you'll notice the traffic from all of the other families going on vacation. If you head north through Nags Head and Duck, you'll get to Corolla. This is the point of seclusion, the road turns into beach and you can keep driving along the beach in your 4x4 alongside natural herds of horses.
